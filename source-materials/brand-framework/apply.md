## Channel-by-Channel Guidelines

### Website
- Cream foundation, dark navy heroes
- Editorial type (one display per page)
- Scroll-spy breadcrumbs for orientation
- "Warm and clear; outcome-focused" messaging

### Social Media
- Navy panels with bright-blue accent rule
- "More conversational and relatable than website"
- No crisis framing; normalizing tone

### Adviser Scripts/Phone
- "Human first. Paced. Listen before advising"
- Acknowledge emotion before options
- Call-sheet template for reference

### Email
- "Progress-positive, brief, and practical"
- Subject lines signal good news, never anxiety
- Single-column at 600px
- Numerals tabular for alignment

### Partner/B2B
- "More formal... but same values apply"
- Outcome-focused language
- Same type/colour system as consumer brand

## House-Style Guide Summary

- **Voice:** "Arrive calm, build confidence, grow energy"
- **Audience:** Match tone to readiness stage
- **Grammar:** Contractions, short sentences, active voice, British English
- **Numbers:** Consistent money/date/time formatting
- **Sensitive language:** "Debt is a circumstance, not a character flaw"
- **A-Z word list:** ~50 preferred/avoided terms

**Decision rule:** "Would a knowledgeable, caring friend say it this way?"
