# Brand Framework — Voice

**Source:** Purpose Media brand framework, Voice section (5 pages)

---

## Brand Personality

**Core positioning:** "Coach and trusted friend" — someone who cares, knows what they're talking about, and helps people help themselves.

**What it's NOT:**
- Not a good friend (too casual for customers in crisis)
- Not an expert (creates power imbalance with capable adults)

**Tonal model:** "Arrive calm. Build confidence. Grow energy."

The warmth-to-energy progression sequences across the customer relationship, starting with reassurance for anxious arrivals and building to confident coaching for those seeing results.

---

## Five Governing Principles

1. **Warm but not soft** — empathetic without pity
2. **Confident but not corporate** — clear authority without institutional coldness
3. **Practical and outcome-focused** — say what will happen, not what might
4. **Customer-centred everywhere** — phone-conversation test applies to all writing
5. **Short sentences. Contractions** — no jargon, preamble, or self-congratulation

## Tonal Variation by Channel

| Channel | Energy | Warmth | Character |
|---|---|---|---|
| Website | 6/10 | 9/10 | Warm, clear, outcome-focused, trust-building |
| Social | 7/10 | 8/10 | Conversational, normalising, de-stigmatising |

## Terminology Guidelines

### Products/Solutions

| Avoid | Use | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Debt management plan/DMP | A plan to bring your payments down | Outcome-focused |
| IVA/Individual Voluntary Arrangement | Legally protected way to clear debt you can't afford | Acronym removed |
| Insolvency | Formal route to clearing debt (avoid where possible) | Reduces stigma |
| Debt consolidation loan | Bringing your payments together | Explains life impact |
| Write-off | Starting fresh/clearing what you can't afford | Reframes as positive |

### People/Relationships

| Avoid | Use | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Creditors | The people you owe money to | Humanises relationship |
| Debtor | Do not use | Dehumanising term |
| Client | Customer | Warmer, less formal distance |
| Case handler/manager | Your adviser/PayPlan contact | Names relationship not role |

### Process/Language

| Avoid | Use | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Case/case reference | Your plan/your account | Dehumanising replaced |
| Onboarding | Getting started/your first steps | Less corporate |
| Affordability assessment | Finding out what you can genuinely afford | Purpose-driven |
| Default/defaulted | Missed payment/fallen behind | Plain human event |
| Repay | Pay back/pay down | Softens obligation framing |
| Credit file/rating | Your credit record | Less intimidating |

### Customer Experience

| Avoid | Use | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Financial difficulties | Difficult time with money/money stress | Normalises experience |
| Struggling | Managing/working through (sparingly) | Reduces othering |

**Client vs customer rule:** Use "customer" in all consumer-facing communications except regulated documentation (FCA mandates, formal agreements, IVA legal paperwork) where "client" may be legally required.

## Four-Stage Journey Messaging

### Stage 1: First Contact

**Emotional register:** Relief first, reassurance second, information third
**Priority message:** "You did the right thing coming here. We'll take it from here."

| Instead of | Say |
|---|---|
| "Welcome to PayPlan. We offer award-winning debt solutions." | "You've taken the first step. Now let's make this straightforward." |
| "Complete our online debt assessment tool to find the right product." | "Tell us about what's going on and we'll help you work out what's possible." |
| "Our advisers discuss IVAs, DMPs, and other debt management options." | "Getting help doesn't have to be complicated. We'll guide you through every step." |
| [Referral] "Thank you for visiting PayPlan. Complete the form below." | [Referral] "Your adviser at [partner] mentioned you'd be getting in touch. We're glad you did." |
| [Chat] "How can we help? Please select a query type from the menu." | [Chat] "Hi. What's going on with your finances? Tell us as much or as little as you like." |

### Stage 2: During a Solution

**Emotional register:** Encouragement and practical calm, progress not process
**Priority message:** "You're doing it. Here's where you stand. Here's what comes next."

| Instead of | Say |
|---|---|
| "Your monthly DMP instalment has been processed successfully." | "You're six months in. Your payments are going out on time and everything is on track." |
| "Your outstanding balance currently stands at £X,XXX." | "One year down. Here's what's changed since you started." |
| "If circumstances change, contact us to discuss a variation." | "Something changed in your finances? Let us know. We can adjust your plan." |
| [Subject] "Monthly payment notification — PayPlan reference #XXXXX" | [Subject] "Your plan is on track. Here's this month's update." |

### Stage 3: Post-Solution

**Emotional register:** Celebration, then forward motion — treat as landmark
**Priority message:** "You did it. Here's what your financial life looks like now."

| Instead of | Say |
|---|---|
| "Your DMP has now been completed. Your account is closed." | "You're debt-free. That is worth taking a moment to acknowledge, genuinely." |
| "If difficulties arise, please contact us." | "This is the start of something different. Let's make sure your finances stay on track." |
| [No post-solution communication] | "A lot of people feel odd after completing a plan, not sure what comes next. That's normal." |

### Stage 4: Ongoing Wellbeing

**Emotional register:** Empowerment and possibility, forward-looking
**Priority message:** "You're in control now. Let's keep it that way."

| Instead of | Say |
|---|---|
| "Debt can come back if you're not careful." | "Budgeting well isn't complicated, it's just a habit. Here's how to build one." |
| "As a former PayPlan customer, we wanted to share money tips." | "Your finances are in good shape. Here's how to make them better." |
| [Tool prompt] "Sign up to our financial monitoring service." | [Tool prompt] "BudgetSmart finds savings in every corner of your finances." |

## Context-Based Do/Don't Library

### Homepage and Primary Entry Points

**Do:**
- "Getting help with debt isn't something to be ashamed of, it's something to be proud of. We've helped over a million people start over."
- "You don't have to have it all figured out to get in touch. Just tell us what's going on, we'll take it from there."
- "Free. Regulated. Genuinely impartial. PayPlan has no product to sell you. Only advice that puts you first."

**Don't:**
- "Are you struggling with debt? Our award-winning debt management solutions can help you regain control."
- "PayPlan is a leading provider of debt management plans and individual voluntary arrangements in the UK."
- "Don't let debt control your life. Act now."

### Explaining Complex Products (e.g. IVA Explainer)

**Do:**
- "An IVA is a legal agreement between you and the people you owe money to. It means you pay back what you can genuinely afford over five or six years, and anything left at the end is written off. It's legally binding, which means your creditors can't chase you once it's in place. This option isn't right for everyone, and we'll always tell you if it isn't right for you."

**Don't:**
- "An Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA) is a form of insolvency and a legally binding agreement between a debtor and their creditors to repay all or part of their debts over a specified period. IVAs are one of the most popular debt solutions in the UK. Find out if you qualify."

### During-Plan Communications

**Do:**
- "You're now six months in. Your payments are going out on time and everything is on track."
- "Something changed with your income or outgoings? Let us know, we can adjust the plan to fit."

**Don't:**
- "Please be advised that your DMP instalment of £XXX has been processed this month."
- "As per the terms of your arrangement, please ensure your payment is received by [date] to avoid a breach of your plan."

### Encouragement to Act (Pre-Engagement, Social, PPC, Content)

**Do:**
- "Most people who call us say the same thing: 'I should have done this sooner.' We're here whenever you're ready."
- "A quick conversation with one of our advisers costs nothing and commits you to nothing. It's just a conversation."

**Don't:**
- "Click here to see if you qualify for debt help."
- "Get a free debt assessment today. Find out which debt solution could reduce your monthly payments by up to 70%."
- "Warning: ignoring debt can make your situation worse. Act now."

## Regulatory Note

Terminology replacements apply where PayPlan has editorial discretion. FCA requirements, Financial Ombudsman Service mandates, formal client agreements, and statutory notices override brand terminology guidelines.
