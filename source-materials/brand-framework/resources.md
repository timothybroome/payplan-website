## Glossary

### Framework Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Brand framework | The single reference point for how PayPlan presents itself (updated regularly, not static) |
| Brand profile | Strategic foundation document (v0.2 current) |
| House-style guide | Operational companion defining exact content rules |

### Strategy Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Brand purpose | "To make it easy for people to take control of their debt without judgement" |
| HEART values | Human, Empowering, Ambitious, Real, Transformative |
| Readiness spectrum | Seven-stage model (pre-aware through post-solution) |
| Eligibility gap | Structural tension addressing three gap types plus a "must still offer a path" principle |

### Voice Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Coach and trusted friend | Brand personality combining care with expertise |
| Tonal model | "Arrive calm. Build confidence. Grow energy" sequencing |
| Terminology | Sector-to-customer-experience vocabulary translation |

### Design Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Fresh | Visual direction (four pillars: typography, colour, layout, imagery) |
| Debt-diary standard | "Real people in real environments, shot in natural light" |
| Inter | Primary typeface from Google Fonts |

### Sector Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Breathing Space | 60-day government debt protection scheme |
| DMP | Debt management plan (refer to function, not acronym) |
| IVA | Individual Voluntary Arrangement (5-6 year debt clearing route) |
| SDRP | Statutory Debt Repayment Plan (anticipated late 2027) |
| BudgetSmart | Financial wellbeing tool |
| FCA | Financial Conduct Authority (UK regulator) |
