## Forms

| Pattern | Usage | Detail |
|---|---|---|
| Multi-step assessment | Self-serve assessment (4 steps) | One question per screen |

## Content Notes from Wireframes

- "Reassurance before product" — explain PayPlan's function plainly first
- Permission-led language (especially early-stage pages)
- Honest about limitations (not all solutions suit everyone)
- No alarm or shame-inducing language
- "One story, web and phone" — messaging aligned across channels
