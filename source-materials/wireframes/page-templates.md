### Primary Navigation (All Pages)

- Where do I start?
- Ways to clear your debt
- Life after debt
- About
- Your plan
- **Get help now** (action button, every page)

- **Get help:** Where do I start? | Ways to clear your debt | Check your options
- **Resources:** Life after debt | Guides and advice | Your plan
- **PayPlan:** About us | Contact | 0800 316 1833
- MoneyHelper reference and FCA credibility markers

## 2. Where Do I Start? (`payplan.com/where-do-i-start`)

**Serves:** Pre-aware and passively aware personas

### Sections

- **Permission-led hero:** "Going through a difficult time with money?"
- **"See where you stand, privately"** — self-serve assessment CTA
- **"Not sure it's bad enough to ask?"** — reassurance with three scenarios:
  - I've missed a payment
  - I'm only paying minimum
  - I'm keeping it to myself
- **"Small things that help right now"** — tools section (Benefits check | Budget builder | Bill savings)
- **Solutions grid** — for those ready to research

**Key attribute:** Non-alarming tone, minimal use of word "debt," focuses on eligibility permission

## 3. Solution Page Template (`payplan.com/ways-to-clear-your-debt/debt-management-plan`)

**Applies to all solution pages (DMP, IVA, DRO, Bankruptcy, Trust Deed, etc.)**

### Header Section

- Breadcrumb navigation
- Solution name as H1
- Plain-English benefit statement
- Dual CTAs: "Start online" and "Talk it through"
- Trust badges: Trustpilot rating, people helped, zero fees, payment allocation percentage

### Content Architecture

1. **"[Solution] at a glance"** — table (Good if | Cost | How long | Worth knowing)
2. **"Been here before?"** — fast-track CTA for known users
3. **"Is a [solution] available to you?"** — two-sided evaluation:
   - "[Solution] may suit you if..." (4 conditions)
   - "Things worth knowing" (4 limitations/risks)
4. **"How it works"** — three-step process (visual)
5. **"Which debts a [solution] can cover"** — inclusion/exclusion lists
6. **"From people who've been here"** — 3 customer testimonials
7. **"Is this right for you?"** — alternatives section linking to other solutions
8. **Comparison table** — [Solution] vs alternatives (legally binding | debt outcome | monthly payments | home impact | public register)
9. **"Common questions"** — accordion with 6+ expandable Q&As

## 4. Self-Serve Assessment (`payplan.com/check-your-options`)

**New build. Serves Priya persona (hidden carriers)**

### Structure

- **Privacy-first messaging:** "No account needed | Private and confidential | Save and come back"
- **Multi-step form** — one question per screen (example shows Step 2 of 4)
- **Example question:** "Roughly how much do you owe in total?" with radio buttons (£<5k | £5k-£20k | £20k-£50k | £50k+)
- **Results screen:** "Thanks for taking this step. From what you've told us, a Debt Management Plan could be a good fit"
- **Explanation** of recommendation outcome
- **Dual CTAs:** "Set this up online" and "Talk it through first"

**Key feature:** No account creation, automated outcome at completion

## 6. Your Plan / Client Area (`payplan.com/your-plan`)

**Serves:** In-plan, sustaining stage (Claire persona)

### Header

- Personalized greeting with logout option

### Progress Dashboard

- "You're on track" reassurance messaging
- Progress metrics: "Paid so far" (amount + payment count) | "Next payment" (amount + due date) | "On current pace" (percentage complete)

### Action Blocks

- **"Manage your plan"** — four actions:
  - Update your details
  - View and change payments
  - Your documents and statements
  - Review your budget
- **"Something changed?"** — adviser contact CTA
- **"Getting close to the end?"** — bridge to life-after-debt experience

**Key attribute:** Elevated from header link to significant destination, emphasizes progress and reassurance

## 8. Partner Landing Page (`payplan.com/natwest`)

**Serves:** Creditor-referred customers (single-purpose conversion)

### Design Approach

Co-branded header with partner logo, **no main navigation**

### Sections

1. **Hero:** "Free, confidential debt help — chosen by NatWest"
2. **Two-question form:** Debt amount slider (£0-£100,000+) + Contact preference
3. **Trust indicators** specific to referred context
4. **"How can it be free?"** explainer
5. **"What happens after you get in touch"** — three-step expectation-setter
6. **Partner-specific FAQs:**
   - Will NatWest see what I tell you?
   - Do I have to stop using my NatWest account?
   - Is this really free?
   - What if a plan isn't right for me?
7. **Customer testimonials** (3 reviews with star ratings)
8. **Phone CTA:** 0800 316 1833

**Key attribute:** Distraction-free, partner-specific messaging, no exploration paths
